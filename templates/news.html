2082 Bhadra 19 / 4 Sep 2025 Awareness

Road Accident Reduction Awareness Program Successfully Concluded

The Kathmandu Valley Traffic Police Office concluded its month-long road safety awareness drive in collaboration with the Department of Roads and partner municipalities. The program reached an estimated 60,000 commuters across 14 checkpoints in the valley, focusing on helmet compliance, lane discipline, and pedestrian right-of-way education.
